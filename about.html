About Verifow

Compliance built for Africa

Verifow is an automated AML/CFT compliance platform designed specifically for banks, fintechs, and lenders operating across Africa. Born from a need for faster, more accurate, and locally deployable compliance infrastructure.

Our Story

Why we built Verifow

Financial institutions in Africa face a unique compliance challenge. Global AML tools are expensive, slow, and built for markets with entirely different regulatory frameworks. Local alternatives are fragmented, requiring banks to stitch together multiple vendors for sanctions screening, KYC verification, and regulatory reporting.

We built Verifow to solve this. A single platform that handles sanctions screening against UN, OFAC, UK HMT, Interpol, and PEP lists; automated CBN rule enforcement; BVN and NIN verification; and the generation of CTR, SAR, and FTR reports, all in under 200 milliseconds per transaction.

Whether you are a tier-1 bank processing millions of transactions daily or a fast-growing fintech launching your first product, Verifow scales with you. Deploy in the cloud for speed, or on-premise for complete data sovereignty.
